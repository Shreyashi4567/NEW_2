OUR STORY

At She Safe, we are committed to empower women by addressing the challenges of safety in public spaces and at the workplace. Our mission: To provide resources, tools, and a community that helps women navigate these challenges with confidence. We believe that every woman deserves to feel secure and respected in all areas of life.

Women's Rights Laws

The Sexual Harassment of Women at Workplace Act, 2013

This act mandates the protection of women against sexual harassment in any workplace. It requires all employers to establish an Internal Complaints Committee (ICC).

The Maternity Benefit Act, 1961

Ensures paid maternity leave for women and mandates creche facilities in establishments with 50 or more employees.

Equal Remuneration Act, 1976

Guarantees equal pay for men and women for the same work without discrimination.

Indian Penal Code (IPC), Section 354

Protects women from acts of assault or criminal force with the intent to outrage modesty.

Prohibition of Eve Teasing (Section 509)

Addresses verbal harassment or indecent gestures towards women in public spaces.

Criminal Law (Amendment) Act, 2013

Introduced stricter penalties for crimes such as acid attacks, stalking, and voyeurism, and established a clearer framework for reporting harassment in public areas.
